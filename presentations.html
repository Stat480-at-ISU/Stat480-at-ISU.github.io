Final presentations



Presentation structure:

  • As a team, you have 1 minute for the intro and 1 min for the conclusions.
  • In addition, each team member has 5 minutes to present one piece of the overall project.
  • Try to balance out the load and make sure that everybody keeps within their assigned time.
  • You will evaluated both as a team and as an individual.


Team evaluations:

  • Introduce and motivate your topic
  • Explain the data
  • Draw general conclusions over-arching all of the individual questions
  • Unify your slides - use the same theme, use similar colors, etc.
  • Practice together! Make sure you are able to get the talk up and running in 1 minute, if you're using additional media, make sure everything is available on the machine you're using, practice hand-overs from one person to the next
  • Make sure that everybody knows what everybody else is talking about to the point that you could answer questions on each others parts.


Individual evaluations:

  • Everybody in the audience will be asked to give feedback on every single presenter.
  • Make sure that your name is visible and legible on every one of your slides, so that people know, who they are commenting on.
  • Have a look at the individual evaluation form for a list of things to consider for your talk.
